How to Play Treasure Map

Objective: Navigate an expedition grid, find treasures to boost your multiplier, and cash out before hitting a trap.

Game Setup:

  • Set your bet amount (MIN or MAX buttons available)
  • Choose your difficulty: Easy (fewer traps), Medium (balanced), or Hard (more treasures, higher risk)
  • Select your map size: 4x4 (16 tiles), 5x5 (25 tiles), or 6x6 (36 tiles)

Gameplay:

  • 💎 Treasure Tiles: Reveal treasures to boost your multiplier significantly
  • ✓ Safe Tiles: Reveal safe water tiles for smaller multiplier gains
  • 💣 Trap Tiles: Hitting a trap ends the expedition immediately with no payout

Winning Strategy:

  • Larger maps take longer to complete but hide more treasures and more traps
  • Your potential win is displayed live: bet amount × current multiplier
  • Cash out anytime to secure your winnings before triggering a trap
  • Higher difficulty means more treasures available for bigger multipliers
