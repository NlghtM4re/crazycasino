Troubleshooting

  • If credits look wrong, refresh the page once before assuming data is lost.
  • If progress resets, your browser storage may have been cleared.
  • If ads do not appear, an ad blocker or browser privacy setting may be hiding them.
  • If a game feels slow, close heavy tabs and try again.
