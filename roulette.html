How to Play Roulette

Goal

  • Predict where the ball will land on the wheel.
  • Place bets on numbers, colors, or groups before spinning.
  • Win credits based on the payout of each bet type.

Bet Types

  • Straight (single number) — pays 35 : 1
  • Red / Black — pays 1 : 1
  • Odd / Even — pays 1 : 1
  • 1–18 / 19–36 — pays 1 : 1
  • Dozen (1st/2nd/3rd 12) — pays 2 : 1
  • Column — pays 2 : 1

How to Bet

  • Set your chip value in the input box.
  • Click any number on the table to bet on it directly.
  • Click outside-bet buttons (Red, Black, Odd…) for group bets.
  • Click the same spot again to remove that bet.

Tips

  • Spread bets across numbers to cover more outcomes.
  • Outside bets (Red/Black) win nearly half the time.
  • Zero (0) wins only on a straight bet on 0.
  • Use Clear Bets to remove all wagers at once.
