How to Play Crash

Goal

  • Start the round and watch the multiplier rise from 1.00x.
  • Cash out before the crash to lock in your payout.
  • If the crash hits first, the whole bet is lost.

Setup

  • Enter your wager or use the quick bet shortcuts.
  • Use Max if you want to stake all available credits.
  • Enable auto bet or auto cashout for repetitive rounds.

During the Round

  • Every second increases the potential payout and the risk.
  • Take Profits cashes out instantly at the live multiplier.
  • The status strip shows whether the round is waiting, counting down, running, or crashed.

Fairness

  • Each round generates a fresh seed and public commit.
  • After the round, the seed and resulting hash are revealed.
  • You can verify that the crash point came from the published data.
